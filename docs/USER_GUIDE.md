# G.A.I.N User Guide

Welcome to G.A.I.N! This guide will help you navigate the AI-powered food detector and nutrition tracker.

## How to Use

### 1. Detect Food
- Click the **"Upload Food Image"** button.
- Select a clear photo of your food.
- The AI will analyze the image and display the top 3 predictions.
- If the AI is confident (>65%), it will automatically select the food and calculate the calories.

### 2. Manual Selection
- If the AI doesn't automatically select the food, use the **"Verify/Select Food"** dropdown to choose the correct item.
- Adjust the **"Serving Multiplier"** if you ate more or less than a standard serving.
- Click **"Calculate Now"** to update the results.

### 3. Track History
- All your detections are saved in the **"Session History"** table at the bottom.
- Your data is saved automatically in your browser, so you can refresh the page without losing your history.
- To clear your history, click **"Reset Session"**.

### 4. Export Data
- **Save Result**: Download the current food's nutrition details as a text file.
- **Export CSV**: Download your entire session history as a spreadsheet-compatible file.

## Health Points System
G.A.I.N rewards healthy choices:
- **Positive Points (+)**: Earned for healthy foods like Idly or Dhokla.
- **Negative Points (-)**: Deducted for less healthy options like Jilabi or Pizza.
- **Goal**: Maintain a high positive balance to demonstrate a healthy lifestyle.

## Settings
- Use the **"Toggle Theme"** button in the top right to switch between Light and Dark modes.
